# **App Name**: LegallyAI

## Core Features:

- Lawyer/Law Firm Onboarding: Streamlined profile creation and onboarding process for lawyers and law firms, highlighting expertise, services, and credentials.
- AI-Assisted Lawyer Recommendations: Utilize AI to match business users with the most suitable lawyers based on their legal needs, industry, and other relevant factors. LLM uses its tool for finding the correct legal expert.
- Real-time Consultation: Enable voice/text consultations between users and lawyers for initial discussions and preliminary advice.
- Secure Engagement Finalization: Provide a secure platform for finalizing engagement terms, agreements, and payment arrangements between users and lawyers.
- Smart Search: Provide an intuitive search feature to quickly find lawyers or law firms based on location, expertise or services.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to create a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) to highlight key actions and information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will provide a blend of modern appeal and readability
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif, for a tech-forward feel in headings
- Use professional and easily recognizable icons for legal services, areas of expertise, and communication features.
- A clean and structured layout is essential, making legal information easily accessible. Focus on readability and clear hierarchy.
